## What is Optifai?

Optifai is the first CRM built as a **System-of-Action**, not a System-of-Record.

Let me explain what that means.

### System-of-Record vs. System-of-Action

**Traditional CRMs (System-of-Record):**
- Philosophy: "Record everything, and value will emerge"
- Designed for: Managers and executives
- User workflow: Open CRM ‚Üí Find information ‚Üí Take action elsewhere
- Input required: Constant manual data entry
- KPI focus: Data completeness, forecast accuracy

**Optifai (System-of-Action):**
- Philosophy: "Automate everything, and humans focus on customers"
- Designed for: Salespeople and revenue teams
- User workflow: Open Optifai ‚Üí AI tells you what to do ‚Üí Execute in 1 click
- Input required: Near-zero
- KPI focus: Revenue Lift per Human Hour

The difference is fundamental. Traditional CRMs ask: *"What happened?"* Optifai asks: *"What should I do next?"*

---

### Our North Star: Revenue Lift per Human Hour

Most CRMs optimize for data completeness. Optifai optimizes for **Revenue Lift per Human Hour**.

This metric captures what actually matters:
- **Revenue Lift**: Incremental revenue generated by the system
- **Human Hour**: Time saved or made more productive

If a tool takes 10 hours of your time and generates $5,000 in revenue, that's $500/hour. If another tool takes 1 hour and generates $2,000, that's $2,000/hour. The second tool is 4√ó better‚Äîeven though it generated less total revenue.

### 2. Real-Time Scoring: AI Priority Engine

**The Problem:**
You open your CRM and see 47 open deals. Which one should you work on first? Most CRMs sort by deal size or close date. But that's not how deals actually work.

**How Optifai Solves It:**

Optifai calculates an **Expected Value (EV)** for every deal in real-time:

```
EV = Deal Size √ó Win Probability √ó Urgency Score
```

But unlike static lead scoring systems that use rigid rules, Optifai's AI analyzes:
- **Engagement patterns**: Email opens, reply speed, meeting attendance
- **Behavioral signals**: Questions asked, objections raised, stakeholders involved
- **Company data**: Industry, size, growth stage, tech stack
- **Historical data**: Similar deals you've won or lost
- **Timing signals**: Budget cycles, contract expirations, competitor activity

The result? You open Optifai, and the top 5 deals you should work on *today* are ranked by EV‚Äînot by your gut feeling or arbitrary rules.

**Example:**

| Deal | Size | Win % | Urgency | EV | Action |
|------|------|-------|---------|-----|--------|
| Acme Corp | $50K | 75% | High | **$37.5K** | Send pricing proposal (drafted by AI) |
| TechStart | $120K | 15% | Low | $18K | Schedule discovery call |
| BuildCo | $30K | 80% | Medium | $24K | Follow up on decision timeline |

Without Optifai, you'd probably chase TechStart because it's the biggest deal. But the math says Acme Corp has **2√ó higher expected value**. Optifai shows you this instantly.

**Real Impact:**
An Optifai customer (15-person SaaS sales team) increased **revenue per rep by 2.3√ó** in 6 months‚Äînot by working harder, but by working on the *right* deals at the *right* time.
